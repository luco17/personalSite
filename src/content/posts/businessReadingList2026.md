| 1 | *Build* | Tony Fadell | Product lessons from Apple and Nest. |

| 7 | *Creative Selection* | Ken Kocienda | Apple’s iterative software and product development. |
| 8 | *Born to Be Wired* | John Malone | Malone’s cable empire and complex dealmaking. |
| 9 | *Cable Cowboy* | Mark Robichaux | Cable economics, leverage, taxes and Malone. |
| 10 | *Four Seasons* | Isadore Sharp | Luxury hospitality, service and asset-light growth. |
| 11 | *Grinding It Out* | Ray Kroc | McDonald’s franchising, standardisation and property model. |
| 12 | *The Outsiders* | William N. Thorndike | Exceptional chief executives allocating capital unconventionally. |
| 13 | *Setting the Table* | Danny Meyer | Restaurant operations, hospitality and employee culture. |
| 14 | *Changing How the World Does Business* | Roger Frock | FedEx’s launch, financing and network struggles. |

| 19 | *Titan* | Ron Chernow | Rockefeller, Standard Oil and industrial consolidation. |
| 20 | *The Box* | Marc Levinson | Containerisation’s operational and economic transformation. |
| 21 | *The Prize* | Daniel Yergin | Oil’s commercial, technological and geopolitical history. |
| 22 | *Big Brown* | Greg Niemann | UPS operations, culture and Casey’s philosophy. |
| 23 | *The Frackers* | Gregory Zuckerman | Entrepreneurs who transformed American oil production. |
| 24 | *The Success Equation* | Michael J. Mauboussin | Separating skill from luck in outcomes. |
| 25 | *The Autobiography of Morris C.M. Chang*, Volumes I–II | Morris C.M. Chang | TSMC founder’s life and semiconductor career. |
| 26 | *Working Backwards* | Colin Bryar and Bill Carr | Amazon mechanisms for execution and innovation. |
