---
title: Margin vs markup
date: 2026-05-05
---

To price something for a target margin, divide the buy cost by `1 - target margin`.

Example:
- buy cost = 100
- target margin = 25%
- sale price = 100 / 0.75 = 133.33

This is margin, not markup.
Markup would add 25% onto cost. Margin is profit as a share of the selling price.
